Helix Labs

Launch-ready brand assets and tradeshow collateral for a next-generation DNA analysis platform

Client
Helix Labs
Industry
Genomics / DNA Analysis
Services
Brand Assets, Tradeshow
Year
2025

The Challenge

Helix Labs had built a breakthrough portable DNA sequencing platform capable of delivering results in under 90 minutes. Their technology was validated, their pipeline was strong, and Series A funding was secured. What they didn't have was a brand presence that matched their ambition.

With their first major product launch approaching and booth space booked at three flagship genomics conferences, Helix Labs needed a complete suite of go-to-market materials. They had weeks, not months, and everything needed to work together seamlessly — from one-pagers handed to investors to 10-foot banners commanding attention on the show floor.

The challenge was clear: translate complex science into collateral that sells, without sacrificing the technical credibility their audience demands.

Our Approach

LYCKA embedded with the Helix Labs team for a focused three-week sprint, working across brand assets and tradeshow collateral simultaneously to meet their launch timeline.

Go-to-Market Brand Assets

From one-pagers to product brochures, we created the collateral Helix Labs needed to launch with confidence. Every asset was designed to reinforce the brand while serving its specific purpose — whether that meant distilling a complex workflow into a single visual for investors, or building a spec sheet that gave procurement teams exactly what they needed to approve a purchase order.

We developed a modular design system that allowed Helix Labs to generate new materials in-house after launch, ensuring brand consistency as their needs scaled.

Tradeshow & Conference Collateral

Standing out at the booth takes more than good science. We designed tradeshow graphics, banners, signage, and leave-behinds that grabbed attention in crowded exhibition halls and kept Helix Labs top-of-mind long after the event ended.

The booth design used bold structural graphics paired with clean data visualizations, creating an environment that invited conversation. Every touchpoint — from the retractable banners to the business cards — reinforced the same narrative: DNA analysis, simplified.

The Impact

Helix Labs launched at AGBT, ASHG, and Genomics Live with a unified brand presence that turned heads and started conversations. The results spoke for themselves.

The brand assets became Helix Labs' primary sales toolkit, with the one-pager alone credited in closing three of their first enterprise deals. Conference attendees consistently commented that the booth "looked like a company ten times their size."

More importantly, the modular design system we built allowed Helix Labs to maintain brand consistency as they expanded into new markets — producing new materials internally without needing to start from scratch each time.
