05 — Usage

Logo Usage Rules

Maintaining consistency across all touchpoints protects brand equity and ensures recognition.

Do

Use the logo on approved brand colors (Brand Teal, white, or black backgrounds). Maintain the minimum clear space. Use the provided vector files for all production.

Don't

Never stretch, rotate, or distort the logo. Do not place it on busy photo backgrounds without a solid backing. Never change the ice-blue accent color.

Do

Use the horizontal lockup for small sizes and tight layouts. Scale the mark and wordmark proportionally. Use the monochrome version when color printing is unavailable.

Don't

Never separate the mark from the wordmark in the lockup. Do not add drop shadows, strokes, or effects. Never use the logo smaller than 24px in height on digital.
