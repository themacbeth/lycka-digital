Modernizing chromatography analysis software for next-generation researchers

Client
ChromaIO Labs
Industry
Lab Software
Services
Brand, Web Design
Year
2024

The Challenge

ChromaIO came to LYCKA with a powerful chromatography analysis platform hampered by outdated branding and a confusing market position. Despite having superior technology, they were losing potential customers to competitors with more approachable brand identities.

The existing visual identity felt clinical and dated, alienating younger researchers while failing to communicate the platform's innovative capabilities. Their website buried key features under technical jargon, and new users faced a steep learning curve that discouraged trial signups.

ChromaIO needed to transform from a legacy tool into a modern platform that felt as innovative as the science it enabled.

Our Approach

LYCKA partnered with ChromaIO to reimagine their brand from the ground up, creating an identity that would resonate with both seasoned chromatographers and early-career researchers.

Brand Identity

We developed a fresh visual system built around the metaphor of data visualization itself. The logo's colorful waveforms reference chromatography peaks while feeling modern and approachable. The color palette balances scientific credibility with accessibility, moving away from sterile blues toward a warm, multi-spectrum approach.

Web Experience

The new website prioritizes clarity and conversion. We restructured the information architecture to lead with benefits rather than features, using clear language that speaks to researcher needs: faster analysis, better collaboration, and confidence in results.

Interactive product demos replace static screenshots, letting visitors experience the platform's capabilities before signing up. The design system scales seamlessly from desktop workstations to mobile devices, recognizing that researchers access tools across contexts.

The Impact

The rebrand transformed ChromaIO's market position overnight. Within three months of launch, the platform saw dramatic improvements across all key metrics.

User satisfaction

The new brand helped ChromaIO secure Series A funding three months ahead of schedule, with investors citing the professional brand presence as a key factor in their decision. The platform was featured in Analytical Chemistry magazine and presented at major industry conferences.

Most importantly, researchers reported that ChromaIO now "feels like software built for scientists by scientists who understand modern design." The brand successfully bridges scientific rigor with contemporary user expectations.
